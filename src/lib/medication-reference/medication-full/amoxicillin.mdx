<Callout>
  A beta-lactam antibiotic (aminopenicillin) commonly used for a wide range of
  bacterial infections. Use according to local susceptibility patterns and
  stewardship principles.
</Callout>

## Dose

### Adults

- 500 mg TDS

## Administration

- Take with or without food.

## Adverse Reactions

- Common: diarrhoea, nausea, rash.
- Serious: severe hypersensitivity (anaphylaxis), Clostridioides difficile infection.

## Pregnancy and Lactation

- Generally considered safe in pregnancy and lactation when indicated for use.
